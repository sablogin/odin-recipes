Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • Heavy cream
  • Whole milk
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Optional: Chocolate chips, fruit, or other mix-ins

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, whole milk, sugar, and vanilla extract until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  3. If adding mix-ins, fold them into the ice cream during the last few minutes of churning.
  4. Transfer the ice cream to a freezer-safe container and freeze for at least 2 hours before serving.
