Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef with chopped onion and garlic until browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce to the skillet and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer the noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
  7. Bake in the oven for 25-30 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
